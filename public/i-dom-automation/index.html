[DIFFICULTY:branching] Branching choice

Three paths. Picking one swaps the sub-view below. Exercises walkthrough branching via offer quick replies or callout buttons.

[DIFFICULTY:viewswap] View switcher

Tab bar swaps the visible content. Aids from the previous view become stale — the walkthrough must re-observe (snapshot) after clicking a tab.

[DIFFICULTY:modal] Modal dialog

Opens a centered overlay with form fields. Tests scrolling, cues on portal content, and the dismiss flow.
